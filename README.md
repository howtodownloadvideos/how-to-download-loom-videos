# How to Download Loom Videos for Free (Chrome Browser Extension Method)


Loom Video Downloader is a browser extension for saving authorized Loom videos from pages you can access.

This guide turns the walkthrough into a clear Chrome setup process.

> Only download Loom videos you own, have permission to save, or are legally allowed to use offline.

## What Is Loom Video Downloader?

Loom Video Downloader detects Loom videos on the current page and gives you a download option from the extension panel or from a button near the video.

Verified repository:

```text
https://github.com/serpapps/loom-video-downloader
```

## Before You Start

You will need:

- Google Chrome.
- A page with a Loom video you are authorized to download.
- The Chrome release asset `loom-downloader-chrome.zip`.
- An email address for one-time verification.

## Step 1: Find the Loom Video Downloader Repository

Search Google for:

```text
serpapps loom downloader
```

Open the GitHub repository for `serpapps/loom-video-downloader`.

## Step 2: Download the Chrome Asset

Open **Releases** and choose the latest release.

For Chrome, download:

```text
loom-downloader-chrome.zip
```

Other verified assets include `loom-downloader-edge.zip` and `loom-downloader-brave.zip`.

## Step 3: Extract the ZIP

Extract the ZIP file. Chrome will load the extracted extension folder.

## Step 4: Open the Extensions Page

Open:

```text
chrome://extensions
```

Turn on **Developer mode**.

## Step 5: Load and Pin the Extension

Click **Load unpacked** and select the extracted folder from `loom-downloader-chrome.zip`.

Then pin Loom Video Downloader from the puzzle-piece extensions menu.

## Step 6: Verify Your Email

Open the extension, enter your email address, send the verification code, and paste the code back into the extension.

After verification, the extension will prompt you to navigate to a page with a Loom video.

## Step 7: Open a Loom Video Page

Go to a page that contains a Loom video. Refresh the page if it was already open.

The extension may show a download button near the video and list the video in the extension panel.

## Step 8: Download the Loom Video

Click **Download Video** in the extension panel, or use the download button on the video itself.

A progress indicator appears while the video downloads.

## Step 9: Find the Downloaded File

When the download finishes, the file saves to your browser's download location, such as Downloads or Desktop.

## Troubleshooting Tips

- Refresh the page after installing and verifying the extension.
- Make sure the page contains a Loom video.
- Check for the video button near the top-right of the video.
- Confirm the asset name is `loom-downloader-chrome.zip`.

## FAQ

### Do I need to play the Loom video first?

Usually refreshing the page is enough, but playing the video can help if the extension does not detect it.

### Can I download from the extension panel or the video button?

Yes. The walkthrough shows both options.

### Where does the video save?

It saves to the default download location configured in your browser.

## Final Thoughts

The Loom method is simple: install `loom-downloader-chrome.zip`, verify your email, open a Loom video page, and click the download button from the page or extension.
